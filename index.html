Welcome to Jabs Recycling

The Modern Age Recycling With Great Skillset

Found in 2020, Jabs Recycling and Waste Management is a black run and controlled South African Company, it is also BEE compliant company in terms of HID determination. The company provides services to a wide range of clientele within the public, private sector. Jabs Recycling and Waste Management is a new start-up company, found and owned by Jeffrey Jabulani Mazibuko (100% shareholder) with over 10 years working experience in senior positions, also a director and OPS Manager to other two companies operating for over 5 years.

Jabulani who is driven by passion and new challenges in the corporate field possesses an advanced certificate in Project Management, Electrical Trade Certificate and other technical training. With a high level of technical experience and expertise working in different industries, the services offered are surely of high quality and guaranteed high efficiency..
